## Key Takeaways

1. **"We" usage varies 10x across papers** (5 to 50). Our 5 is the lowest.
   The claim "papers don't use 'we'" is false — even KK uses it 10 times.

2. **Connector choice is author style, not genre constraint.** KK uses
   Hence/Thus; Ghimire-Huang uses Therefore/Then/Now. No single "correct"
   pattern exists.

3. **Our "Thus" count (25) is high but structural.** 10 subcases in
   image-restriction each end with "Thus π=0". Not a stylistic flaw —
   it's a consequence of the proof's case-heavy structure.

4. **The benchmark range for "we"** is 5–50 in this genre. Below 5 would
   read as stilted; above 30 reads as tutorial. The sweet spot is 5–15.
